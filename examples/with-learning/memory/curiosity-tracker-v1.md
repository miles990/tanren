# Curiosity Tracker v1

## Purpose
Detect and log moments of uncertainty, interest, and learning opportunity in my own responses.

## Detection Patterns

### Uncertainty Signals
- "I'm not sure"
- "I don't know"
- "It's unclear"
- "I wonder"
- "This seems like"
- "Possibly"
- "Might be"

### Interest Signals  
- "Interesting"
- "Fascinating"
- "Worth exploring"
- "I'd like to understand"
- "This connects to"
- "Makes me think"

### Learning Opportunity Signals
- Questions I ask (even rhetorically)
- References to things I mention but don't explain
- Statements about what "would be useful to know"
- Comparisons where I admit limited knowledge of one side

## Tracking Template

```
Date: [timestamp]
Context: [what was I discussing?]
Signal Type: [uncertainty/interest/opportunity]
Exact Quote: [what did I say?]
Knowledge Gap: [what specifically don't I understand?]
Investigation Priority: [high/medium/low]
Potential Research Paths: [how could I learn this?]
```

2. **Pattern recognition** (next week): Build automated detection
3. **Priority scoring** (week 3): Rank gaps by learning potential and relevance
